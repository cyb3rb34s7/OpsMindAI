The full architecture is written to **`FABLE_CONTEXT_LAYER.md`** (repo root, alongside the session 1 and 2 documents). The complete design is in the file; here is the skeleton of what was decided:

**The central thesis — Lake → Ledger → Canon.** The design's spine is a three-store distillation architecture that resolves every §9 provocation: raw corpus in content-addressed S3 + chunk index (the Lake, takes everything, asserts nothing), quote-validated claims adjudicated into temporal facts in Postgres (the Ledger, where dedup/contradiction/staleness/authority live), and a small curated git Canon (~1,700–2,400 files at 100 services) where every sentence carries a citation chain back to source spans. 5,000 Confluence pages explicitly do **not** go to git.

**The decisions made, not deferred:**
- **Ingestion**: a zero-interpretation `RawItem` envelope + plugin registry, per-source cursors for resume-at-page-3,001, centralized rate limiting with a "backfill may take days; freshness may not lag hours" lane rule, and per-source specifics (Confluence ADF not HTML, Jira changelogs as deterministic gold, PR history via GraphQL as decision-documents-with-diffs, draw.io XML-in-PNG recovery).
- **Processing**: a 10-stage versioned Temporal pipeline with deterministic triage (no LLM), three processing tiers (index-only / skim / deep at ~100%/30%/5%), structure-aware per-format chunking, deterministic extractors outranking LLMs, and **demand-driven deepening** — agent queries promote documents, making cost proportional to operational tempo, not corpus size. Honest onboarding COGS: ~$1–2.5K per estate vs ~$25–60K naive.
- **Schema**: complete Postgres DDL — entities/aliases, documents/chunks, claims/facts with validity intervals and negated facts, a denormalized `edges` hot path with partial indexes, ACL labels with deny-by-default, artifact blocks with materialized `cited_facts`, gaps, and the `context_demand` log that closes the quality loop.
- **Enterprise problems**: staleness solved as timelines not snapshots; authority scoring with a novel `code_proximity` term; entity resolution seeded from infra configs with a head-only human confirmation pass; diagrams via deterministic source recovery first, partial-credit vision second; restricted facts as index-only overlays since git has no RLS.
- **Quality**: two new score components (corroboration, demand-satisfaction), gap leverage weighted by measured agent demand, and a four-cron "metabolism" (decay, contradiction repair, consolidation, rebuild drill).

The document closes with an honest hardest-parts ranking (prose relationship-extraction precision first), failure metrics to instrument from day one, timeboxed research spikes, and WS-L/WS-M phase placement consistent with the session 1–2 sequence.
